Core Objective of the ISCAA-LEONIX EV Project

To demonstrate the synergy of innovative chassis design, advanced electronic power management, and real-world data analysis to achieve superior on-track results in a competitive engineering context. The ISCAA-LEONIX EV project is our team's key initiative to push the boundaries of electric vehicle technology.

Chassis Innovation: Carbon Fiber & Aerodynamics

Our approach leveraged an ultra-lightweight and exceptionally rigid carbon fiber monocoque chassis, combined with a low-drag body shell optimized through computational fluid dynamics (CFD).

Team Structure & Skill Development

The LEONIX team is organized into key departments, each contributing specialized expertise to the project's success. This collaborative structure enhances both technical and soft skills across the board.
